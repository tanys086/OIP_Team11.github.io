Why LiDAR?

Solution Comparison

Technology Comparison

01

Precision & Accuracy

LiDAR provides millimeter-level accuracy in distance measurement, enabling precise visitor counting even in crowded conditions.

02

Heritage Compatibility

Non-contact sensing technology requires no structural modifications, making it ideal for Category A Listed buildings.

03

Environmental Resilience

Operates reliably in varying light conditions and humidity levels typical of glasshouse environments.

04

Privacy Compliant

Measures distance and movement without capturing identifiable personal information, ensuring visitor privacy.
