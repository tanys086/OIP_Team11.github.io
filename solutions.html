Physical Prototype

Cardboard Prototype Development

Our hands-on cardboard prototype demonstrates the physical implementation of our LiDAR solution within the Kibble Palace environment. This tangible model allows stakeholders to visualize sensor placement, mounting mechanisms, and aesthetic integration without requiring expensive hardware or risking damage to the heritage building.

How It Works

Our solution leverages LiDAR technology, which detects the contours of objects within its field of view. By establishing an invisible threshold at the center of this view, we can precisely monitor movement across that line allowing us to track individuals entering and exiting the space in real time.

Privacy

Our solution does not capture or store any personally identifiable information. LiDAR only detects object contours, ensuring that individuals remain anonymous while enabling effective visitor flow monitoring in a privacy-respecting manner.

Sample Use Cases

Daily Operations Management

Operational

Scenario:

Glasgow City Council staff need to optimize staffing levels and visitor services throughout the day at the Kibble Palace.

Our Solution:

Real-time dashboard displays current visitor counts and predicts peak times based on historical data. Staff receive alerts when visitor capacity approaches optimal levels, enabling proactive service adjustments and crowd management.

Benefits:

  • Improved visitor experience through optimized staffing
  • Reduced operational costs through efficient resource allocation
  • Data-driven decision making for service improvements

Event Planning & Impact Assessment

Strategic

Scenario:

The Friends of Glasgow Botanic Gardens plan special exhibitions and events but lack data on visitor response and optimal event sizing.

Our Solution:

Historical analytics show visitor pattern changes during events, helping organizers understand attendance impacts. Comparative reporting reveals which events generate sustained interest versus one-time visits.

Benefits:

  • Evidence-based event planning and marketing strategies
  • Optimal event sizing to maximize engagement without overcrowding
  • ROI measurement for special programs and exhibitions

Heritage Conservation Support

Conservation

Scenario:

Conservation experts need evidence of visitor impact on the historic building and plant collections to inform preservation strategies.

Our Solution:

Long-term visitor trend analysis correlates with environmental monitoring to identify usage patterns that may affect conservation. Data supports funding applications and preservation planning with concrete visitor impact evidence.

Benefits:

  • Evidence-based conservation planning and resource allocation
  • Enhanced funding applications with concrete usage data
  • Balanced approach to accessibility and preservation

Phase 1: Pilot Installation

Phase 2: Full Deployment

  • Advanced analytics feature activation
